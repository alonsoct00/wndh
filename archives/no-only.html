No Only

written & composed by Lane Dombois directed by and developed with Nic Adams co-developed with Fernando Gonzalez and Sophie Zmorrod

Synopsis

No Only is a musical about how perception and affection change environments—from the subatomic particles comprising living cells to astronomic objects pulling and pushing one another through space. One character, Dust—an explorer and romantic—mourns the collapse of the earth. Wind encounters her and interrogates her mourning. Yet resistance to his cold questioning brings a radical environmental shift, forcing both Wind and Dust to reinterpret what it means to be and not be in an unpredictable, cruel universe. Without the tools for a neat and comprehensive interpretation, without the autonomy to control how time evolves—they are left only to each other's ideas and hopes. What use are ideas and hopes, when the end is inevitably coming?
