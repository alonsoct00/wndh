Synopsis

The Chimes - A young married couple have their lives and relationship inflicted by the torment of a neighbor's incessant and omnipresent wind chimes. Though they confront the neighbor, the chimes' music won't dissipate, prodding at the dormant wounds that lie deep within them.

Love in the Dark - When an orthodox Jewish man betrays the trust of his marriage and his devotion to his faith by seeking out a prostitute to whet his sexual frustration, he is forced to rediscover his identity and redefine his perception of his submissive wife
