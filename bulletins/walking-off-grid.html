Walking, Off-Grid, a Smaller Island

June 23, 2023

And finally No Only comes to a venue again. Not the apartment of old, but: a shipping container powered by an off-grid solar/storage system.

The container belongs to Brooklyn-local cultural organization caribBEING–it is the caribBEING House, and WNDH is indebted to Shelley Worrell and co. for their generosity in donating the space as part of their residency on Governor's Island, NYC.

In August, 2023, Lane will be hosting tours of No Only. Attendees aill cross a channel to the island from either Manhattan or Long Island, accompanying their journey with the introduction of the two protagonists of No Only: Dust (Sophie Zmorrod) and Wind (Fernando Gonzalez).

And to the shipping container--designed by Leonie, Brian, and Christina, with the work of costume designers Sandra Bell, Inga Black, and Cinti Salandy–the journey will take us, where design is upheld by batteries and the end of the story and world awaits. The experience is being built. Watch for what's Current.
