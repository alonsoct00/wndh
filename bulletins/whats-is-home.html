"What is Home?", a whale might also ask

May 7, 2022

To just let things be earnest, maybe overly: things of great importance, the most profound importance–they don't ever really announce themselves. An announcement is suspect. Training to discern magic in mundaneity is the reunlearning of feeling the soul slip out of nights.

Ana and Lane grew eager to hear a moment of sisterly playful sister solemnity from Submersible, and contacted singers Diana Huey and Sophie Zmorrod to record "What is Home?" at the Creamery studio in Greenpoint, Brooklyn. Ken Helmlinger engineered, Diana as Andee, Sophie as her sister Marni, and Ana played the upright piano. Also couldn't help but conduct.

The demo was made for authorial ears. Ana and Lane continue the long migration north. Though–are you sure this is the way?
